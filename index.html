Our Story

15 Years of Coming Together.

The George Barnes Family Reunion began in 2012 with a simple but powerful vision. Mike Hicks, seeing how life and distance had scattered family members across the country, decided something had to be done. He wanted to bring everyone back together — not just for a day, but to build a tradition that would carry the family forward for generations.

What started as a gathering has grown into something much bigger. The reunion has become the place where cousins meet for the first time, where elders share stories that would otherwise be lost, and where the youngest members of the family learn who they are and where they come from. Even during the pandemic, the family stayed connected — gathering on Zoom to keep the tradition alive.

In 2026, we celebrate 15 years of reunion. This milestone belongs to every family member who has shown up, contributed, and kept the spirit alive — and to Mike, whose vision made it all possible.
